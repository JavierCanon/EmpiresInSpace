The files should be 
Unicode (UTF-16) , tab-separated and Text-Divider " (double quotation mark)


I am using Libre Office Calc to modify the file(s). 
When saving, Choose "Save as", and activate the checkbox "filtersettings" (actually "Filtereinstellungen bearbeiten" in german).
Replace the old file, and in then new dialog select:
Unicode
Tab
Text-Divider "

The line with "Text zwischen Hochkommas ausgeben" should not be checked
